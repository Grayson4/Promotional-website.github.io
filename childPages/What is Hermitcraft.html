What is Hermitcraft

What is Hermitcraft. Hermitcraft is a private Minecraft server with 26 active member wich they call Hermits. This server is a survival multiplayer made for YouTubers and twitch streamers to colaberat and make content. On the Hermitcraft server there are multipule seasons as they call it, for every new season they choose an new Minecraft map to make it a fresh new start.Currently there is 8 completed seasons, and the 9th is in progress with all of the members making new videos on it. In the server they are all very talented builders and they make these amazing bases all in their own styles (some photos below).

What is Hermitcraft

What is Hermitcraft. Hermitcraft is a private Minecraft server with 26 active member wich they call Hermits. This server is a survival multiplayer made for YouTubers and twitch streamers. On the Hermitcraft server there are multipule seasons as they call it, for every new season they choose an new Minecraft map to make it a fresh new start. In the server they are all very talented builders and they make these amazing bases all in their own styles (some photos below).
